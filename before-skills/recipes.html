Spaghetti Bolognese

⏱ 45 min 👥 4 servings 🟡 Medium

A hearty Italian meat sauce slow-cooked with tomatoes and aromatics over al dente spaghetti.

View Recipe
Ingredients
  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil
Steps
  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  2. Add ground beef and brown well.
  3. Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, and oregano.
  4. Simmer on low for 25 minutes, season to taste.
  5. Cook spaghetti per package directions; serve with sauce.

Butter Chicken

⏱ 50 min 👥 4 servings 🟡 Medium

Tender chicken in a rich, creamy tomato-based curry sauce with warming Indian spices.

View Recipe
Ingredients
  • 700g chicken thighs, cubed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, 4 garlic cloves, 1-inch ginger
Steps
  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, half the garam masala, and turmeric for 30 min.
  2. Sear chicken in butter until lightly charred; set aside.
  3. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger; add remaining spices.
  4. Add tomatoes; simmer 10 min, then blend sauce until smooth.
  5. Return chicken to sauce, stir in cream, and simmer 10 min.

Greek Salad

⏱ 10 min 👥 2 servings 🟢 Easy

A refreshing Mediterranean salad with crisp vegetables, olives, and creamy feta cheese.

View Recipe
Ingredients
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup kalamata olives
  • 150g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
Steps
  1. Combine tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and olives in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk together olive oil, vinegar, and oregano.
  3. Drizzle dressing over salad and toss gently.
  4. Top with feta cheese and serve immediately.

Chicken Ramen

⏱ 40 min 👥 2 servings 🟡 Medium

A warming Japanese noodle soup with a savory broth, soft-boiled egg, and tender chicken.

View Recipe
Ingredients
  • 2 packs ramen noodles
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 tsp sesame oil, nori sheets
Steps
  1. Simmer chicken breasts in broth 20 min; remove and slice.
  2. Whisk miso paste and soy sauce into broth; add sesame oil.
  3. Cook ramen noodles per package directions.
  4. Divide noodles into bowls, ladle hot broth over them.
  5. Top with sliced chicken, halved soft-boiled egg, green onions, and nori.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning

Margherita Pizza

⏱ 35 min 👥 2 servings 🟡 Medium

A classic Neapolitan pizza topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil.

View Recipe
Ingredients
  • 250g pizza dough
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 250°C (480°F) with a baking stone or sheet inside.
  2. Roll dough into a 30cm circle on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce, leaving a 2cm border for the crust.
  4. Arrange mozzarella slices on top; drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake 10–12 min until crust is golden. Top with fresh basil.

Vegetable Curry

⏱ 35 min 👥 4 servings 🟢 Easy

A vibrant, plant-based curry packed with seasonal vegetables in a coconut milk sauce.

View Recipe
Ingredients
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp red curry paste
  • 1 zucchini, 1 bell pepper, 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 onion, 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt, lime juice, fresh cilantro
  • Cooked rice, to serve
Steps
  1. Sauté onion and garlic in oil over medium heat.
  2. Add curry paste and stir 1 min until fragrant.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Add all vegetables and chickpeas; cook 15 min until tender.
  5. Season with salt and lime juice; serve over rice with cilantro.

Fluffy Pancakes

⏱ 20 min 👥 2 servings 🟢 Easy

Light and airy American-style pancakes perfect for a weekend breakfast, served with maple syrup.

View Recipe
Ingredients
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¾ cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Maple syrup and fresh berries, to serve
Steps
  1. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda.
  2. In another bowl, mix buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Fold wet ingredients into dry until just combined (lumps are fine).
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium; pour ¼ cup batter per pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form (~2 min), flip, and cook 1 min more. Serve with syrup.

French Onion Soup

⏱ 1 hr 15 min 👥 4 servings 🟡 Medium

A classic French bistro soup with deeply caramelized onions topped with crusty bread and melted Gruyère.

View Recipe
Ingredients
  • 1kg yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 litre beef broth
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme
  • 4 slices baguette, toasted
  • 1½ cups Gruyère cheese, grated
Steps
  1. Melt butter with oil in a large pot; add onions and cook on low 45–50 min, stirring often, until deep golden.
  2. Deglaze with white wine; cook until absorbed.
  3. Add broth and thyme; simmer 15 min. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Ladle soup into oven-safe bowls; float a baguette slice on top.
  5. Cover with Gruyère and broil until bubbly and golden.

Chocolate Lava Cake

⏱ 25 min 👥 4 servings 🟡 Medium

Indulgent individual chocolate cakes with a warm, gooey molten center — a restaurant-worthy dessert at home.

View Recipe
Ingredients
  • 170g dark chocolate (70%), chopped
  • 115g unsalted butter, plus extra for ramekins
  • 2 eggs + 2 egg yolks
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Cocoa powder, for dusting
  • Vanilla ice cream, to serve
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Butter and dust 4 ramekins with cocoa.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together; let cool slightly.
  3. Whisk eggs, yolks, and sugar until pale; fold in chocolate mixture.
  4. Fold in flour and salt until just combined.
  5. Divide batter among ramekins; bake 12 min until edges are set but centers jiggle. Serve immediately with ice cream.
